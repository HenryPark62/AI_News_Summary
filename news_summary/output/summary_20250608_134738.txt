김 위원장 intends to resolve the issue with former president Yoon by this week. There is a growing voice within the party that demands a clean break with Yoon for the sake of expanding the moderate base. Kim Mun-su, a presidential candidate, finds it difficult to take a leading role in this issue. The decision to leave the party rests with Yoon himself, and the Blue House is currently not in communication with him. There is internal resistance to the idea of Yoon leaving the party. Some argue that Yoon's personal popularity could hinder those who seek power in the future.

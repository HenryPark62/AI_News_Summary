김 위원장 intends to resolve the issue of the party's relationship with former President Yoon within this week. There are discussions about requiring Yoon's resignation, but the party has not yet taken a clear stance. Opinions are divided within the party, and there is pressure from members and supporters to address Yoon's continued membership. Meanwhile, Kim Mun-soo, former New Frontier Party leader, has virtually ordered Yoon to resign. The Blue House has stated that Yoon will make the decision about his resignation himself.
